How it works?

Pressing the top button start the timer starts running
By pressing the stop button timer stops, leaving the elapsed time displayed.
A press of the reset button resets the stopwatch to zero.

Stop watch

The most logical reason for emphasizing the "stop" in a watch
that can be both started and stopped is that it is the "stop"
that is the part in which people are really interested.When
using a stop watch, a person wants to know how long something
takes, with some degree of accuracy.Starting the watch is
easy enough.
